Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that everyone loves!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Layer ingredients in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.
