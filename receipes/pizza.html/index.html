Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza is cheesy, delicious, and easy to make. Perfect for any pizza lover!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a lightly floured surface into a round shape.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Top with your choice of toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, or anything you like.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the top and sprinkle with oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!
